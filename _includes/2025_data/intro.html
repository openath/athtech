#AthTech25

AthTech, the Athletics Technology Conference, is a meeting place for all the people working behind the scenes to make the sport more enjoyable and easier to manage through technology.

Come and hear from World and European athletics, from member federations, race organisers and service providers, to be at the cutting edge of the sport!
